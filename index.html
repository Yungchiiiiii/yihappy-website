關於我們 About Us

倚樂企業有限公司

Yi Happy Co., Ltd.

倚樂企業有限公司是一家專注於數位印刷及教科書印刷製作的專業公司。我們以精湛的技術與高品質的服務，協助出版社、學校及企業客戶將文字與圖像完美呈現於紙上。

Yi Happy Co., Ltd. is a professional company specializing in digital printing and educational textbook production. With advanced technology and high-quality service, we help publishers, schools, and corporate clients bring their content to life on the printed page.

我們深信每一本書、每一份教材都承載著知識與價值，因此在每一個印刷環節都追求最高標準，確保最終成品達到客戶的期待。

We believe that every book and educational material carries knowledge and value, which is why we uphold the highest standards at every stage of the printing process.

我們提供的服務

What We Offer

從數位印刷到教科書製作，我們提供一站式的專業印刷服務，滿足您所有的印刷需求。

From digital printing to textbook production, we offer end-to-end professional printing services to meet all your needs.

數位印刷
Digital Printing

採用最新數位印刷技術，提供高解析度、色彩精準的印刷成品。適合各種數量需求，小批量亦可快速交付，節省成本與時間。

Utilizing the latest digital printing technology to deliver high-resolution, color-accurate printed products. Suitable for any quantity with fast turnaround even for small runs.

教科書印刷
Textbook Printing

專業的教科書印刷製作服務，從版面設計到裝訂完成，確保每一本教科書的品質達到教育出版標準，讓學習材料更加耐用美觀。

Professional textbook printing from layout to binding, ensuring every textbook meets educational publishing standards — durable and well-presented.

教材製作
Educational Materials

協助學校與補習班製作各類教學講義、練習本、試題卷等教學輔助材料，提供客製化尺寸與裝訂方式，滿足不同教學需求。

Helping schools and tutoring centers produce teaching handouts, workbooks, and test papers with custom sizes and binding options.

大批量印刷
Bulk Printing

針對出版社及大型機構提供大批量印刷服務，以穩定的品質管控與有競爭力的價格，確保每一份訂單都準時完成交付。

Bulk printing services for publishers and large organizations, with consistent quality control and competitive pricing — delivered on time.
